Organizes One Week Workshop

"Embracing Diversity: Expanding Research Perspectives in Computer Science"

8th-12th July 2024 (Hybrid) Register Now
OBJECTIVES

AI and Data Science in Engineering

The objective of this extensive AI-based workshop is to provide participants with a comprehensive understanding of AI and Data Science in Engineering.

Key Areas of Focus

It covers essential areas like dataset acquisition, deep learning for healthcare analytics, generative AI for computer vision, distributed ML modeling with Spark, and UAVs for disaster management and precision agriculture.

Hands-on Sessions

Participants will also engage in hands-on sessions on Generative AI Technology and Bibliometrics Analysis.

Equipping for the Future

equipping them with the tools to excel in the evolving field of AI-driven solutions.
