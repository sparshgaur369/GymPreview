OBJECTIVES

Introduction

The objective of this extensive AI-based workshop is to equip participants with a thorough understanding of the application of artificial intelligence (AI) and data science in engineering disciplines. By exploring key areas such as dataset acquisition, deep learning for healthcare analytics, generative AI for computer vision, distributed machine learning (ML) modeling with Spark, and the utilization of unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) for disaster management and precision agriculture, participants will gain valuable insights into the transformative potential of AI-driven solutions in engineering.


Key Focus Areas:


Dataset Acquisition

Participants will delve into the critical process of dataset acquisition, where they will learn techniques for gathering, preprocessing, and managing datasets essential for training AI models. Understanding data acquisition processes is fundamental for ensuring the quality and relevance of data used in engineering applications.

Deep Learning for Healthcare Analytics

In this segment, participants will focus on leveraging deep learning techniques for analyzing healthcare data. Through practical exercises, they will explore how deep learning models can be applied to tasks such as disease diagnosis, medical image analysis, and patient outcome prediction. These insights contribute to advancements in healthcare delivery and patient care.

Generative AI for Computer Vision

Participants will explore the exciting realm of generative AI, where they will learn how to generate realistic images and manipulate visual content using advanced AI models. Through hands-on sessions, they will gain insights into the creative potential of generative AI techniques in engineering applications, such as image synthesis and enhancement.

Distributed ML Modeling with Spark

The workshop covers distributed machine learning modeling using Apache Spark, a powerful framework for processing large-scale datasets. Participants will gain practical experience in building and deploying ML models in distributed computing environments, enabling them to tackle complex engineering problems efficiently and effectively.

UAVs for Disaster Management and Precision Agriculture

This segment examines the use of UAVs in disaster management and precision agriculture. Participants will learn how UAVs can be equipped with sensors and AI algorithms to provide real-time data for disaster response and agricultural optimization. This highlights the role of AI-driven technologies in addressing societal and environmental challenges.

Hands-on Sessions

Participants will engage in hands-on sessions focused on generative AI technology and bibliometrics analysis. These sessions will provide practical experience and tools to excel in the evolving field of AI-driven solutions. Through interactive exercises, participants will develop the skills and knowledge needed to apply AI and data science techniques effectively in engineering contexts.
